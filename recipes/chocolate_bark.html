Chocolate Bark For When You're Skint And Need To Bring A Gift

So easy even the worst chef can make this

Ingredients

  • As much chocolate as you can get your hands on, any type
  • Toppings, for example dried fruit, nuts, seeds, pretzels, anything yummy except olives and chicken

What to do

  1. Melt the chocolate using your chocolate melting technique of choice
  2. Spread out the chocolate on a tray covered with greaseproof paper
  3. Sprinkle toppings over the chocolate while it is still melted
  4. Allow to set in the fridge before breaking into pieces
