Delicious Lasagne

Learn how to make this delicious lasagne below. I don't measure anything so just eyball it and make sure it looks nice.

Ingredients

  • Some beef mince
  • A few lasagne sheets
  • Some chopped onion
  • A jar of passata/bottle of passata
  • Maybe some tomato puree
  • Add some grated vegetables if you feel like it
  • Lots of garlic if you aren't suffering from covid-induced parosmia
  • You can add herbs if you like. Oregano, rosemary, basil are all good. Maybe avoid star anise.
  • Don't forget oil, whatever you have to hand
  • Cheese sauce - no recipe for this as it always goes wrong when I make it
  • Cheese for the top

What to do

  1. Preheat the oven to quite hot
  2. Fry onion, beef and garlic (if using) until it looks nice
  3. Add passata and tomato puree and any other vegetables
  4. Cook down until it looks even nicer
  5. Layer the lasagne sheets, mince mixture and cheese sauce in a dish in a way that looks pretty
  6. Top with cheese
  7. Bake until it looks good
  8. Eat it. Maybe not all at once. Unless you just made one portion
